The Irishman

The Irishman movie is about a truck driver who gets involved in the Italian mob, taking place in the mid-20th-century in Philadelphia. The movie is sad and violent, based on the best selling book, "I Heard You Paint House" by Charles Brandt. For being one of their best hitman at the time, the trucker Frank Sheeran works under the Boss Russell for his organized crime family the Bufalino's. Frank is introduced to very famous and political people that worked with his organized crime family, one of these characters is the Teamster leader Jimmy Hoffa, who he ends up being really close friends with during the movie.

Frank Sheeran, Jimmy Hoffa, Russell Bufalino.
